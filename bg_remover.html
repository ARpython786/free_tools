Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool really free to use?

Yes! Our basic background removal service is completely free with no hidden charges. We offer premium features for professional users, but the core functionality is available at no cost.

What image formats do you support?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, JPEG, and WEBP. The processed images are always downloaded as PNG to preserve transparency.

Is there a file size limit?

The free version supports images up to 5MB. For larger files up to 25MB, consider our Pro version which also offers higher resolution outputs.

How do you handle my uploaded images?

Your privacy is important to us. All images are processed in memory and automatically deleted from our servers after 1 hour. We never use your images for any other purpose.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Absolutely! You retain full rights to all images you process with our tool. Feel free to use them in any personal or commercial projects.
